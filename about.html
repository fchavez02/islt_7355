My name is Felipe Chavez, and I currently teach mathematics at Metea Valley High School in Aurora, Illinois. I have been a teacher at this school for four years. As a high school teacher, I plan and deliver well thought out and engaging mathematical lesson plans to help students of all various learning styles and needs.

Education

My educational experience begins with my undergraduate studies at Aurora University in Aurora, Illinois. At Aurora University, I graduated in 2020 with a Bachelor of Science with a focus on Mathematics Secondary Education. I am currently enrolled in the Learning Technologies and Design Master’s program at Mizzou University in Columbia, Missouri. I plan to graduate from Mizzou University with a master’s degree by next summer. The reason for studying this program is to improve my learning of instructional design to apply to various aspects of my career as a teacher.
